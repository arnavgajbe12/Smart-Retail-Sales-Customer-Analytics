## 📌 Project Overview  
This project analyzes a smart retail store's sales, customer behavior, product performance, and inventory efficiency using **Python, SQL, and Data Analytics techniques**.  
It is designed to showcase end‑to‑end real‑world analytical skills for Data Analyst roles.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Identify **top‑selling products**  
- Analyze **customer demographics & buying patterns**  
- Generate **key KPIs** (Revenue, Avg Order Value, Repeat Rate)  
- Build **SQL reports** for management  
- Provide **Business Insights + Recommendations**

---

## 🔍 Key Insights (Sample)  
- 20% of products generate 65% of sales (Pareto effect)  
- Electronics category drives the highest revenue  
- Loyalty customers purchase **2.4× more** than new customers  
- Inventory stockouts caused **₹1.2L+ revenue loss**

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations  
✔ Stock fast-moving items aggressively  
✔ Launch targeted marketing for "Young Professionals"  
✔ Improve inventory forecasting  
✔ Bundle high-demand product pairs to maximize profits
